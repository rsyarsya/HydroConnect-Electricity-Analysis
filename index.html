Key Insights & Recommendations

  • Exceptional Capacity Growth: The project achieved a 567% increase in power capacity, from 450W to 3000W potential, ensuring robust operations without overload issues.
  • Energy Independence: Solar panels generate 257% more energy than daily consumption (5.15 kWh vs 2.0 kWh), creating significant energy surplus for storage and future expansion.
  • Monitoring Revolution: Transition from manual periodic monitoring to real-time IoT system monitoring 4 critical water quality parameters (TDS, Turbidity, pH, Temperature), enabling proactive maintenance.
  • System Resilience: 3-hour backup capability ensures continuous operation during PLN outages or low solar conditions, critical for community water security.
  • Optimization Opportunity: With 61% energy surplus, there's potential for expanding services or adding community facilities like EV charging stations or additional water treatment units.
  • Sustainability Impact: The hybrid system reduces PLN dependency by approximately 78%, significantly lowering operational costs and carbon footprint.
